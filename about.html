SALMOOKIES

Family own business using local and organic products. We took Salmon, one of Seattle's signature icons and made it into delicious and nutritious cookies! Perfect for Seattle's weather.

We are a family friendly business and believe that family is the most important thing, and that is why we give baking classes to kids and parents! This is a very cool experience. We also believe in creativity and we offer design workshops to learn about graphics and Salmon!
